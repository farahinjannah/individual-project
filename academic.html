MY ACADEMIC PAGE

From Standard 1 to Standard 6 (2009-2014), I attended Sekolah Kebangsaan Padang Tengku or better known as SKPT. I got good results during UPSR which is 4A 1B. I also held the position of School Prefect from Standard 4 to Standard 6.

In 2015 to 2019, when I was in Form 1 to Form 5, I studied at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Padang Tengku or also known as SMKPT. I got excellent results when PT3. I got 5A. I also obtained SPM 5A results. The elective subjects I took to sit for the SPM exam were Agricultural Science, Computer Science, Tasawwur Islam and Geograhy.

After I finished my SPM, I continued my studies to Form 6 at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Seri Lipis or its abbreviated name, SEMSEL. I took the subjects of General Studies, Bahasa Melayu, Business Studies and Economics. The period I studied in Form 6 was longer than usual, due to the Covid-19 Pandemic. I studied for 2 years. Despite the many challenges learned during the Pandemic era, I managed to get 3.50 CGPA for overall results for all semesters.

In 2023, I have continued my studies to a bachelor's degree, in Information Science at UiTM Kelantan Branch Machang Campus. I also a Secretariat of Jawatankuasa Perwakilan Kolej Tunku Abdul Rahman.
